Welcome to my website!

Rui has a Ph.D. in Civil and Environmental Engineering from Utah State University and an M.S. in Hydraulic and Civil Engineering from Xinjiang Agricultural University. Rui specializes in hydrology, water resources, remote sensing, and data science. Rui’s research focuses on water use estimation via physical- and non-physical-based approaches at different spatial scales. Rui is currently more focused on crop water status and stress estimation using physical- and data-driven-based approaches based on high-resolution UAV images at the field scale (e.g., California vineyards). Rui is also active in professional service as a reviewer for journals like Remote Sensing of Environment and Agricultural Water Management. He is passionate about developing innovative solutions for precise, sustainable, systemic agricultural water management.
